Our Story

Oscar Furniture was founded on a simple belief: a well-made piece should be both beautiful and invisible — so perfectly suited to your life that you stop noticing it and simply start living better because of it.

We work with sustainably sourced oak, walnut, and ash, shaped by craftspeople who understand that restraint is its own kind of skill. Every joint, edge, and surface is considered.
